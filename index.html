CURATING LIFE’S MOST PRECIOUS EXPERIENCES INTO
A BEAUTIFUL & SEARCHABLE ARCHIVE
VIEW DEMO USE OUR API VISIT GITHUB

Pictures can never truly save the gorgeous spontaneity of life. Most of your most precious experiences happen without you expecting it. In order to capture these moments, we created RemembrAll, a Google Glass application that streams video and audio in real time. Our parallelized backend infrastructure analyzes videos frame by frame and transcribes audio to tag each second with rich, structured information within 5 minutes of capture. We then power a web application with fuzzy search to rediscover this content on your computer and compose journal entries and articles in a way that was never before possible.

ABOUT

RemembrAll is a service that tags any video with the audible and visual content in the video and then allows the user to search through the video by typing in keywords, returning the exact point in the video where the keywords match the content.

One of top ten hacks at Canada's Largest International Hackathon

The name for our project was inspired by the magical instrument that goes by the same name in the Harry Potter series written by J.K. Rowling. In the magical world of Harry Potter, a Remembrall is a tennis ball-sized glass ball that contains smoke that turns red when its owner has forgotten something. It turns clear once whatever was forgotten is remembered.
